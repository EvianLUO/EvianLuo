## About Me

I am Yiyun (Evian) Luo, a Ph.D. candidate in Entrepreneurship at City University of Hong Kong and Zhejiang University. My research focuses on the intersection of entrepreneurship, digitalization, and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on how emerging technologies shape entrepreneur and new venture behavior and strategy.

## Education

* **Ph.D. in Strategy Management/Entrepreneurship**, Zhejiang University (2021–2026)
* **B.A. in Business Administration**, Sun Yat-Sen University (2017–2021)
